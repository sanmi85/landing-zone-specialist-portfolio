## Purpose

The purpose of an identity audit is to evaluate the current Identity and Access Management (IAM) architecture in order to identify security risks, 
misconfigurations, and governance gaps.This includes reviewing who has access to cloud resources, what permissions and authorizations have been granted,
 and whether access controls follow the principle of least privilege.
